Cockroach Poker

When you spot one cockroach at home, it means more are on their way.

This is a variant of the original game using a 52-deck of playing card.

Setup

  • 4 players (recommended): deal a hand of 13 cards each.
  • 5 players: 10 cards each (discard the extra 2 cards face up).
  • 3 players: 15 cards each (discard the extra 7 cards face down).

Winning Conditions

The game ends when any player meets one of the following conditions:

  • They accumulate three cards of the same rank.
  • They accumulate four cards of the same suit.
  • It is their turn to play, but they have no cards left in hand.
